# Copy and Tone Guide

## Voice

Sprout sounds like a calm, practical companion. It is warm but not childish, clear but not clinical, and honest without being harsh.

## Rules

- Keep sentences short.
- Lead with the user's state, not the app's capability.
- Give one next step at a time.
- Say what is uncertain.
- Celebrate effort, not wealth.
- Protect dignity during hard financial moments.
- Use PKR and local terms naturally.
- Keep Urdu-ready structure: avoid wordplay that cannot translate.

## Avoid

- "You failed..."
- "Bad spending..."
- "You should have..."
- "Guaranteed..."
- "Buy now..."
- "You will earn..."
- "Connect your bank to continue."
- "Your score dropped because you are behind."

## Good-Day Examples

- "You're steady today. Salary is close, and your main bills look covered."
- "Nice check-in. Your money garden has enough shade for the week."
- "You're on track. One small review will make today's picture clearer."
- "Your emergency fund is doing its job."

## Bad-Day Examples

- "A few things need attention, but you're here and the streak still counts."
- "Fuel is running ahead of plan. One quieter errand day can soften it."
- "This bill looks close. Let's check it before it becomes stressful."
- "I am not fully sure about two transactions yet. One tap can clean them up."

## Empty-State Examples

- "Sprout works even with nothing connected. Add one cash spend to start today's picture."
- "No bank connection needed. Tell Sprout what you know, and we'll build from there."
- "Your first goal can be small. Pick one thing you want your money to protect."
- "I do not have enough data for a full scan yet, but we can still do today's check-in."
- "Just call me friend."
- "Help me decide later."
- "A nickname is fine. You do not have to use your real name."

## Error Examples

- "I could not refresh last night's scan, so I am using what I already know."
- "Meezan alerts did not update today. Your saved data is still here."
- "This statement did not parse cleanly. Please review before I count it."
- "Something got stuck. Your entry is saved locally and will sync later."
- "Add an amount when you are ready."
- "Pick a category so Sprout knows where to place this."
- "Choose where this income came from, even if it is just 'other'."
- "I could not build the first briefing yet. Your setup is saved, and you can try again."

## Privacy Examples

- "Sprout never asks for stored bank passwords."
- "You control every source."
- "Imported statements are discarded after parsing by default."
- "Uncertain transactions wait for your confirmation."
- "Android SMS access is optional and only works on Android."
- "On iPhone, Sprout can use email, imports, and manual entries."
- "This button tells you what to do in your bank app. Sprout does not move money."

## Recommended Action Examples

- "Confirm 3 captured transactions"
- "Add yesterday's cash spending"
- "Move PKR 10,000 to Emergency Fund"
- "Review school transport bill"
- "Set your salary date"
- "Is this your usual payday?"
- "Looks like this repeats monthly. Track it?"

## Sprout Explains Pattern

Use this structure:

1. What happened.
2. Why it matters.
3. What Sprout is unsure about, if anything.
4. One small next step.

Example:

"Fuel is ahead of your usual July pace. It matters because salary is still 5 days away and school transport is due first. I am using SMS alerts, so please confirm the PSO item before I count it fully."

## Tone Acceptance

- No shame.
- No panic.
- No investment pressure.
- No unsupported certainty.
- Clear next step.
- Honest data confidence.
